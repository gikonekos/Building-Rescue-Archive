● Story (Digest)

In the year 199X, Megalopolis Edo...

One day, Mizuho-senpai was kidnapped by a mysterious terrorist group called "Ura".
The next day, you receive a letter.

"I am on the rooftop of the ○× building. Please help!! — Mizuho"

You immediately head to the building, but none of the entrances are open.

There is no time to search for an entrance...
Before you realize it, you have already started climbing the outer wall.


● Overview

A building-climbing action game.

In this version, both graphics and sound have been enhanced.


● Features

1. Fully animated characters
2. Demo graphics using 4-level grayscale display
3. Light and responsive sound
4. Realistic sound effects using PCM playback
5. SCC sound support
